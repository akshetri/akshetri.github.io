    My current research focuses on the topics of quantum computing, quantum simulation, tensor networks and condensed matter physics. This includes developing algorithms for simulating existing quantum hardwares or finding potentially useful applications, developing state-of-the-art tensor network methods to investigate difficult condensed matter problems on non-equilibrium systems, open quantum systems, frustrated quantum systems, real quantum materials, topological phases, etc. The following is a list of my publications.
