About Me

I am a quantum scientist. I develop advanced computational methods. I was previously an Assistant professor at Saha Institute of Nuclear Physics, Kolkata and Homi Bhabha National Institute, Mumbai. Prior to that I was in the group of Prof. Jens Eisert as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Freie University Berlin and Helmholtz Zentrum, Berlin. I did my PhD with Prof. Roman Orus at University of Mainz, Germany after obtaining my Masters at King's College London, United Kingdom.
